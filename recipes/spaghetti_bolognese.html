Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil

Steps:

  1. Saut√© vegetables in olive oil until soft
  2. Add beef and brown completely
  3. Stir in tomatoes, paste, and wine, simmer 1 hour
  4. Cook pasta al dente, reserve 1 cup pasta water
  5. Combine sauce with drained pasta
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan
