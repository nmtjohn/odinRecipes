Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Steps:

  1. Season chicken and cook until golden, slice
  2. Melt butter, saut√© garlic 1 minute
  3. Add cream and simmer 5 minutes
  4. Whisk in Parmesan until smooth
  5. Cook pasta al dente, drain
  6. Toss pasta with sauce and chicken
  7. Garnish with parsley and extra cheese
