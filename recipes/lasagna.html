Lasagna

A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

    Ingredients and steps to make Lasagna

  • Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or pre-boiled)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce or marinara
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Onion (diced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook ground beef with diced onions and garlic until browned
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasonings, simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with 1 egg and parsley (optional)
  5. Layer in 9x13 dish:
    • Thin meat sauce layer
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella/Parmesan
    • Repeat 2-3 times
  6. Top with remaining cheese
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
  8. Uncover and bake 15 more minutes until bubbly
  9. Let rest 10 minutes before serving
